The giant cried, “Fee-fi-fo-fum, I smell the blood of an Englishman. Be he alive, or be he dead, I’ll grind his bones to make my bread!” The wife said, “There is no boy in here!” So, the giant took out his sacks of gold coins, counted them and kept them aside. Then he went to sleep. Jack crept out and took one sack of gold coins.
